Communities

Neocities

Neocities is a modern recreation of what Geocities once was, before it closed in 2009. Geocities was a web hosting service where users could make an account and upload their own websites, for free! A lot of people had accounts with them in the 90s and early 2000s, and the company largely made money via plastering advertisements on the user-created sites. Neocities does not do this,allowing you to upload your site for free with no ads! You can pay for a premium service where you can add custom domains and some other stuff, but it's entirely superfluous if you just want to make a cool little site. I recommend giving some of the most popular pages a browse!

Nekoweb

Nekoweb is very similar to Neocities. Lots of overlap in userbases, and they both serve a very similar purpose. There's a weird thing with them hating on Neocities for whatever reason, but it seems constrained to some small portion of the userbase. Most people on here are cool and friendly!

FARK

FARK is an old-as-shit forum for discussing humorous current events. It's kinda weird, and its culture doesn't necessarily revolve around Web 1.0 stuff, but I would imagine the people active on there have been around for a decade or two.
