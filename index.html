> Welcome to Pipertext

The Web today is a boring place. It's full of uninspired design, propogated largely by the corporations who have overtaken its existence. A once mesmerizing world of creativity and novelty is now a lame dystopia, a mere pop-up simulator.

Here at Pipertext, the goal is to pay respects to the once beautiful Internet. Despite myself not living through the so-called golden age, I have a penchant for the aesthetic and overall "community" that once seemed to dwell these lands. This site is made with those days of old in mind.
